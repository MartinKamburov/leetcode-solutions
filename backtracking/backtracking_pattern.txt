Almost all backtracking problems can be broken down into these 4 steps:
    1. Base Case

    2. Choices

    3. Constraints

    4. Backtracking Step


PseudoCode of Backtracking solutions:

def backtrack(params):
    if base_case_condition:
        results.append(copy_of_solution)

    for choice in choices:
        if violates_constraints:
            continue

        make_choice
        backtrack(updated_params)
        undo_choice # backtracking step
